Featured Hairstyles

Faded Buzzcut

Popularized by figures like Travis Kelce, this low-maintenance cut involves a buzzed head with a skin fade on the sides and back. It’s versatile and suits many face shapes.

Layered Shag

Ideal for men with wavy or curly hair, this style features long layers that enhance natural texture. Think of Timothée Chalamet’s hairstyle in Dune.

Textured Fade

This cut includes a skin fade on the sides and back with short, texturized hair on top. It’s practical and stylish, suitable for various face shapes.

Messy Pompadour

A more casual take on the traditional pompadour, this style maintains volume and height but with a less polished, more relaxed finish.

Curly Quiff

Perfect for curly-haired men, this cut keeps the sides tight while leaving enough length on top to showcase natural curls. It’s often styled with curl cream or pomade for definition.

Side Parting

This classic style is updated for 2024 with longer lengths and a more relaxed, swept side part, reminiscent of Leonardo DiCaprio in Titanic.
